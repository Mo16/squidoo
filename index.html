About us

Squidoo invites you on an amazing journey with their ocean friends to a world where the possibilities are endless. It's all about having fun, being happy, spreading positivity, and helping keep marine habitats preserved. They have come together to help make their home in the ocean cleaner and lead the way to the Squidoverse. Squidooverse will become an interactive hub in the future where the community will be able to interact in real life and the metaverse.
